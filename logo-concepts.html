Momentum Gathering

v5 — Exploring the Momentum Pinwheel · 15 Variations

Spinning, picking up speed, warmth at the core · Inspired by Claude + Granola mark energy

01

Classic Four

Clean four-blade pinwheel with graduated opacity — you can see it spinning. The lightest blade is just catching up.

02

Three Flames

Three organic flame shapes spinning — fewer blades, more energy per petal. Triangular balance feels dynamic and grounded.

03

The Acceleration

Five blades getting progressively bigger — you can literally see the momentum building. From whisper to roar.

04

Bold Swoosh

Thick, confident curved strokes — fewer details, more weight. Feels like it was stamped, not drawn. Gravitas meets motion.

05

Spark Turbine

Slender, pointed blades with a bright spark at center — more electric, more ignition. The turbine IS the spark.

06

The Sketch

The same pinwheel drawn by hand — wobbly, imperfect, alive. Like Granola's mark: deliberate imperfection signals authenticity.

07

The Orbit

Dots in orbit, each growing as they accelerate around center — gathering mass, gathering speed. Gravity as connection.

08

The Spiral

Blades that curve outward in a spiral — like a galaxy spinning. Tight at the center, expanding as they go. Growth in motion.

09

Sealed Momentum

The pinwheel enclosed in a seal ring — motion contained with intention. Energy with authority. Spark meets stamp.

10

Comet Trails

Three points whipping around center, each trailing a gradient tail — you see the motion blur. Speed made visible.

11

The Windmill

Flat, geometric blades with a slight lean — modernist and clean. Reads instantly as motion. The simplest expression of the idea.

12

The Unfurl

Petals positioned along a golden spiral — each one larger than the last, unfolding naturally. Growth that follows nature's own blueprint.

13

The Brushstroke

One bold stroke that curls nearly full circle — fading in as it accelerates. A single gesture that captures momentum. Zen meets energy.

14

The Double Spin

Two pinwheels interleaved — salmon and cream dancing together. Depth, dimension, two forces merging into one rhythm.

15

The M Spin

Two arching forms that spin upward and meet at a peak — reads as both momentum in motion AND an abstract M. Monogram meets mark.
